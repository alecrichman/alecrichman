ABOUT

Lost in Space Camp is a math rock band from Roanoka, Virginia. Composed of guitarist and vocalist Austin Nicely, bassist Bailey Mann, and drummer Ben Nicely, Lost in Space Camp combines twinkly-ness, an unhealthy collection of effects pedals, and a little pretentiousness into an original, if self-indulgent, sound.

Founded in 2016, Lost in Space Camp regularly plays at the hottest Roanoke venus, including Austin's shed, various friends' houses, and occasionally houses of relative strangers. Lost in Space Camp's dedicated fans number in the dozens.
